1. The “action” attribute in a form tag specifies the URL where the form data will be sent upon submission

2. the “method” attribute defines how the data is sent

3. “POST” sends data securely in the request body, making it invisible in the URL, while “GET” appends data to the URL, making it visible and suitable for less sensitive information.
