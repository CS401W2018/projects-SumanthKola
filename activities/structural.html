Engagement Assignment

  1. What goes in the header?
    > The Navigation bar with the links can go here and also the header doesn't move or change.
  2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?
    > The links. Yes it can go in the header. It doesn't have to go inside the header it can go after the header tags. Typically not.
  3. What goes in Main?
    > The actual content of the website goes in Main tag.
  4. What goes in the Footer?
    > The other links to different places in the webiste, legal notice, privacy notice etc.
  5. HTML Validation
    > This is where you put your html code to check errors and validate it.
