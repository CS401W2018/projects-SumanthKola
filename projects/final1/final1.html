About the WTC

World Test Cricket is one of the important formats happens in Cricket. One test is played upto 5days by a both teams. Usually there are 3 or 5 match series which takes about a month to declare who wins a test series. To give more meaning and preference to test cricket, from 2019 the ICC declared this champions trophy and will be played by the top 5 nations in the world. learn more abou it here ICC Official Website.
