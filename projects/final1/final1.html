Cricket History!

The ICC Test Championship, also known as the ICC World Test Championship (WTC), is the premier tournament for Test cricket, introduced by the International Cricket Council (ICC) to establish a global Test champion. Launched in 2019, the WTC was created to give context and meaning to Test matches by creating a structured competition that spans two-year cycles, leading to a final showdown between the top two teams.
