Here's about my missionary service!

India Bengaluru mission

Namasthe, my name is Sumanth Kola from India and I've been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints my whole life. Growing up I always wanted to go and serve a full-time mission and when I was 19 years old I was called to be a missionary in India Bengaluru Mission(IBM), English speaking mission. Interestingly, IBM is my home mission so I served in different district and stakes around me but not in my home stake. It was definitely the best two years to my life!

My mission is pretty huge which covers whole south India which consists of 6 states and neighboring country Sri Lanka. Each state in south India speaks different languages so I learned other state language where I served to have basic communication with people around me. Although, it was needed to learn some basic phrases to communicate I was required to teach the lessons in English only. I had two mission presidents during my time in that mission. We had nearly 250 missionaries from different parts of the world namely U.S, Australia, England, Switcherland, Tonga, and ofcourse the locals from India. I also served in different leadership positions on my missions including:

  1. District leader
  2. Zone Leader
  3. Assistant to the President (AP)
