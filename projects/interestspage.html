Interests Hero

It is the no.1 popular sport in India and no.2 in the world. Although it is not famous in the American continents but it is in rest of the world. I love this sport because this game is unique unlike other sports which have two courts. This is also a mind game and includes a lot of physical reflexes.

I played a lot of cricket growing up in India. I started when I was very young with my cousins and friends at school. I played cricket for my school at sports meet which happens once a year. And even now the Indian community at BYUH we play at PCC ground and sometimes at Kahuku baseball field.
